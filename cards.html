Sylhet

In northeastern Bengal, on the bank of the River Surma, sits the lovely city of Sylhet in Bangladesh. It serves as the district and divisional headquarters for the districts of Sylhet, Moulvibazar, Habiganj, and Sunamganj.

Saint Martin

Being the only coral island in Bangladesh, the tiny enclave of Saint Martin is unique to nowhere else in the country. The area is surrounded by coconut groves and is covered with changing sands and lapping waters.

Bandarban

Bandarban is a city in southern Bangladesh. With hills and lakes that will leave you with lovely memories, it is also a well-liked destination for hikers and trekkers. This region is traversed by the Sangu River, and at least thirteen different indigenous groups reside here.
